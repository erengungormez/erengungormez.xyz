About

Who is that Eren Güngörmez?

I'm Eren Güngörmez, a 2nd-year student in Computer Programming at Bursa Uludag University. I have been immersed in the world of computers since my childhood. Actively entering the software world in 2023, I have connected with many individuals in various software fields, participated in competitions and projects, and achieved success along the way. Currently, I am working towards establishing my own initiative and striving to generate income as a freelancer.
